Clinical & Life Partner
Co-practitioner and co-author. Together we have built a practice that integrates the full spectrum of this medicine across two decades of shared clinical work.

My clinical practice began in 2003 — first in Southeast Asia, then across the Caribbean and North America. From the outset I was drawn to the places where orthodox TCM theory intersected with older, more classical frameworks: the constitutional patterns that do not change, the environmental forces that shape every decision we make.

The Feng Shui work came from the same root. High-net-worth families and developers began inviting me — and my clinical and life partner Van Lam — to audit their homes and commercial projects. For many years this work was done quietly, without formal fee, as part of a deeper relationship of trust. That changed when I understood what I had actually built: a methodology that protects significant decisions.

The proprietary framework I developed — Quantum Elemental Medicine™ — integrates TCM, Classical Feng Shui, constitutional nutrition, and metaphysics into a unified Heaven–Earth–Human model. It is not a metaphor. It is a diagnostic system with clinical and spatial applications tested across thousands of cases and two decades of practice.

My work is not for everyone. I work with people who take their health, their environment, and their decisions seriously — people who understand that the most expensive mistakes are the ones you almost made.
